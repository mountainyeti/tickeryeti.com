What is TickerYeti?

TickerYeti is a stock research tool built for investors who want answers fast. Enter a ticker symbol and get a full research dashboard — no account needed, no paywall, no ads.

One ticker. Instant dashboard. Everything in one place.

Why TickerYeti?

Research time is expensive. Before diving deep into any stock, you need a quick read on the fundamentals — and then you need to open half a dozen sources to dig further.

TickerYeti solves both problems:

  • The dashboard gives you a snapshot of the financials, valuation, and price history before you do anything else.
  • The Research Avalanche button opens every source you need — Yahoo Finance, Seeking Alpha, SEC EDGAR, Finviz — in new tabs in one click, instead of opening each one manually and searching for the ticker.

What data does it show?

Price & Chart

Live price history from Yahoo Finance. 1Y, 2Y, 3Y, 5Y, and 10Y views. Daily close price with volume bars.

Financial Statements

3 years of income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow data — tabbed and easy to scan.

Key Metrics & Valuation

Market cap, P/E, P/B, P/S, current ratio, debt/equity, short interest, and more.

Peers & Research Avalanche

Industry peers you can click to load instantly. One-click Research Avalanche opens Yahoo Finance, Seeking Alpha, SEC EDGAR, and Finviz in new tabs.
